Hi, my name is Alexa, and I'm your personal assistant!
To invoke a command, type `Alexa, `, followed by the command and any other arguments.
The following is a list of commands:
```diff
+help
-Show this message
+info
-Get information about Alexa
+simon says
-Repeat your message
+add
+subtract
+multiply
+divide
-Do math
+weather in
-Get the weather report
+make me
-Colorize your name
+reset my color
-Reset your color role
+time
-Get the time
+date
-Get the date
+super alexa
-Activate Super Alexa mode
+translate
-Translate a word into another language
+roll dice
+flip coin
-Random numbers
+ping
-Get Alexa's latency
+rickroll
-Play Rick Astley in a voice channel
```
